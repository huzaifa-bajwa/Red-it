About

Welcome to our Chrome web extension designed to enhance your browsing experience on any article! Our extension offers a range of powerful features to help you make the most out of your reading. From generating summaries, flashcards, and presentation content to keeping track of your browsing history, translating content into different languages, and providing contextual meanings for words and phrases within articles, we've got you covered. Plus, with our convenient login/signup functionality, you can personalize your experience and access your saved data seamlessly. Upgrade your reading experience today with our comprehensive extension!
